# Adapting Competitive Analysis

**What this skill does:** maps a competitive landscape (5P framework, competitor matrix,
SWOT, feature benchmarking, positioning map) and turns it into prioritized strategic
recommendations, framed against your own position.

This skill is generic methodology — the competitor research is filled entirely from sourced
findings and needs no organization specifics. Only the *our-side* of the comparison ("our
position", "our opportunity") depends on who you are. Decide the values below and either keep
them in your analysis brief at use time, or bake them into your forked copy of `SKILL.md`.
Until they're decided, the skill labels the our-position column `US (PLACEHOLDER)` rather than
inventing differentiators, pricing, or positioning.

## What to decide for your organization

| Decide | Used in `SKILL.md` under | Notes |
|--------|--------------------------|-------|
| **Our offering** — the product or service this analysis is for | Our-position inputs; Output `For:` | One line. |
| **Differentiators** — 3-5 things that make you genuinely different | Our-position inputs, Workflow (Synthesize), Cross-Skill note | Seed the `US` SWOT strengths and the "Our Opportunity" framing. |
| **Key messages** — the 3-9 promises you lead with | Our-position inputs | Inform the positioning recommendation. |
| **Promise** — your core value statement | Our-position inputs | — |
| **Target market / ICP** — the segments or tiers you serve | Our-position inputs; matrix `Target market` row | — |
| **Business model** — how you make money (subscription, usage-based, services, one-time) | Our-position inputs; matrix `Price model` row | No exact figures needed. |
| **Pricing posture** — premium / value / freemium relative to the market | Output (matrix, recommendations) | Posture only, not a price list. |

## How to gather these

The numbered **Our-Position Intake** in [`forms.md`](forms.md) is the questionnaire for all of
the above — ask it once, record the answers wherever your team keeps positioning decisions.
Only fill in what's missing; the competitor side never needs them.

## Platform-independent (do not change)

The competitive method is universal and needs no adaptation:

- `SKILL.md` frameworks (5P, competitor categories, core rules, anti-patterns, workflow).
- `forms.md` Output Template and Quality Checklist.
- `reference.md` research methodology, hidden-competitor tactics, depth tiers, feature
  benchmarking, positioning maps, and SWOT discipline.

These work as-is for any organization, product, or client engagement.
