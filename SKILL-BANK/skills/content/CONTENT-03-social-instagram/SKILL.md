# Instagram Content

Creates Instagram content for a brand account and its associated founder/personal
accounts: reel scripts (the primary format), post captions, stories, carousels, and
profile bios. Instagram is the **energy and personality** platform — less text, more
visuals, more humanity. This skill is the **method**. The specifics it operates on —
the actual voice, content pillars, account roster, taglines, and hashtags — are an
organization's, not part of the skill. See [`ADAPT.md`](ADAPT.md) for what to decide
before using this for a given organization.

> "Visual-first, energy-driven. Less text, more proof. Show what staying ahead looks like."

When those specifics are not yet decided, **do not invent them** — produce a
clearly-labeled **GENERIC DRAFT** with neutral `PLACEHOLDER` values (e.g. `[BRAND]`,
`[@account]`, `[#BrandTag]`) and list what is missing. **Never invent** a tagline, voice,
hashtags, founder names, or account handles.

## Instagram Content Types

| Type | Purpose | Frequency | Best For |
|------|---------|-----------|----------|
| **Reels (15–60s)** | Primary format — energy, personality, reach | 3–5/week (ongoing) | Podcast clips, hot takes, BTS, trending audio |
| **Carousel Posts** | Educational, visual breakdowns | 1–2/week | Frameworks, listicles, before/after |
| **Single Image** | Quote cards, announcements | As needed | Contrarian takes, milestones |
| **Stories** | Daily touchpoints, polls, BTS | Daily when active | Quick polls, day-in-the-life, link sharing |
| **Static Post** | Photo + caption | 1–2/week | Team photos, event coverage, behind-the-scenes |

## Reel Script Format

Reels are the highest-reach format. Every reel follows this structure:

```
[HOOK — 0-3 seconds]
The most important line. Stop the scroll. One sentence.

[BODY — 3-25 seconds]
The main point. Keep it conversational.
One idea per reel. Not three. One.

[CLOSE — 25-30 seconds]
Land the message. One punchy takeaway.
Optional soft CTA: "Follow for more" or "What do you think?"

[END CARD — 2 seconds]
Brand logo + tagline (use the brand tagline exactly).
```

### Reel Rules

| Rule | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Length** | 30 seconds max (60s only for podcast clips with high energy) |
| **Format** | Vertical (9:16), 1080×1920px |
| **Subtitles** | Always — most viewers watch on mute |
| **Audio** | Trending audio when relevant; original audio for to-camera |
| **Cuts** | Fast — new visual every 3–5 seconds minimum |
| **Brand** | Brand color overlays, logo on end card, subtitle styling in brand colors |
| **Tone** | Conversational, direct, energetic — NOT a presentation (match the brand voice attributes) |
| **One idea** | One clear point per reel. If it needs two — make two reels. |

### Podcast-to-Reels Pipeline

1 podcast recording → 1 full episode (long-form video) + 3 reels:

| Reel | Length | What to Clip |
|------|--------|-------------|
| **Reel 1** (Best Moment) | 60s | The most quotable/engaging moment from the episode |
| **Reel 2** (Punchy Take) | 30s | A single hot take or contrarian opinion |
| **Reel 3** (Quick Hit) | 15s | A one-liner or reaction — pure shareability |

Post reels on both the brand account and the relevant founder's personal account
(handles and voices come from your account roster).

## Caption Guidelines

| Element | Rule |
|---------|------|
| **Length** | 150–300 characters max — the reel does the talking |
| **First line** | Hook — but shorter than long-form. One sentence. |
| **Body** | 1–2 sentences that add context the reel doesn't cover |
| **CTA** | Soft — a question, or "Follow [brand handle]" |
| **Hashtags** | 5–10 relevant hashtags, always at the END, never inline |
| **Emojis** | 1–3 max — keep it premium, not cluttered |
| **Mentions** | Tag the co-founder in collab content; tag the brand handle from personal accounts |

### Caption Patterns (fill voice/messaging from your brand decisions)

**For a contrarian reel**:
```
"[One-line contrarian claim drawn from a key message]"

[Acknowledge the discomfort + one supporting proof point.]

What's your take? 👇

[2–4 brand + industry hashtags]
```

**For a podcast-clip reel**:
```
[One reactive line about the clip] 🎙️

Full episode → link in bio

[2–3 brand + industry hashtags]
```

**For a behind-the-scenes reel**:
```
What a [day] actually looks like at [brand].

No filters. No script. Just the work. 🛠️

[2–3 brand + content-pillar hashtags]
```

## Story Content

Stories are for daily, informal touchpoints:

| Story Type | Use | Format |
|------------|-----|--------|
| **Poll** | Quick engagement — "This or That?" | IG native poll sticker |
| **Question Box** | "Ask me anything about [topic]" | IG native question sticker |
| **Behind-the-Scenes** | Phone camera, informal, raw | Photo or 15s video |
| **Reshare** | Share a reel, post, or mention | Reshare with comment overlay |
| **Link Share** | Drive traffic to blog, podcast, or website | Link sticker + brief context |
| **Countdown** | Upcoming event, launch, podcast episode | Countdown sticker |

### Story Rules
- **No heavy production** — stories should feel casual and real
- **1–3 stories per session** — don't overwhelm
- **Use stickers** — polls, questions, countdowns drive engagement
- **Brand colors** — use the brand palette colors for typed text/backgrounds

## Hashtag Strategy

| Category | Source |
|----------|--------|
| **Brand** | The brand and tagline hashtags — always included |
| **Industry** | Sector hashtags aligned to your content pillars |
| **Niche** | Narrow topic hashtags aligned to your key messages |
| **Trending** | Add 2–3 trending/relevant hashtags per post (check Explore) |
| **Location** | City/region hashtags when relevant (from market context) |

**Rules**: 5–10 per post. Brand hashtags always included. Mix broad + niche.
Full category lists live in [`forms.md`](forms.md) — fill the values from your brand decisions.

## Visual Standards

Resolve all colors, logo files, and typography from your brand decisions (apply the
brand-guidelines method). General Instagram standards:

| Element | Standard |
|---------|----------|
| **Colors** | Brand palette only |
| **Logo** | On every designed asset — end card of reels, bottom-right on images |
| **Typography** | Clean sans-serif. Premium. Not cluttered. (the brand display/body fonts) |
| **Photos** | Real, not stock. Casual beats polished for IG. |
| **Grid Aesthetic** | Consistent color theme across posts — cohesive on a profile visit |

## Account-Specific Rules

Each account's voice and content focus come from your account roster (the brand account
plus any founder/personal accounts).

| Account | Voice | Content Focus |
|---------|-------|---------------|
| **Brand account** | "We" — company voice, professional but energetic | Company reels, podcast clips, team content, industry hot takes |
| **Founder accounts** | "I" — personal, reflective, in the founder's voice/angle | Founder reels, day-in-the-life, methodology hot takes, podcast clips |

## Anti-Patterns

| Don't | Instead |
|-------|---------|
| Post text-heavy images | Keep text under ~10 words on visuals |
| Use stock photos | Use real photos or clean graphics |
| Write long-form-length captions | 150–300 chars max |
| Skip subtitles on reels | Always add subtitles |
| Post without hashtags | 5–10 relevant hashtags per post |
| Over-produce stories | Keep stories raw and informal |
| Ignore trending audio | Use trending sounds when they fit |
| Invent a tagline / handle / hashtag | Use your decided values; produce a GENERIC DRAFT if absent |
