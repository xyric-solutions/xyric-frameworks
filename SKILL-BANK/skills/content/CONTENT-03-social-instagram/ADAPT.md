# Adapting Instagram Content

**What this skill does:** creates Instagram content — reel scripts, captions, stories,
carousels, and bios — in an organization's voice and visual brand, across the brand
account and any founder/personal accounts.

This skill is generic methodology — it never hardcodes a specific brand, voice, account,
or hashtag. To use it for your organization, decide the values below and either keep them
in your content brief at use time, or bake them into your forked copy of `SKILL.md`.
Until they're decided, the skill emits a labeled GENERIC DRAFT (with `[BRAND]`,
`[@account]`, `[#BrandTag]` placeholders) rather than inventing them.

## What to decide for your organization

| Decide | Used in `SKILL.md` under | Notes |
|--------|--------------------------|-------|
| **Tagline & wordmark** — exact strings incl. punctuation | Reel Script Format (end card), Anti-Patterns | Used verbatim on reel end cards; never modify or translate. |
| **Voice attributes** — 3–5 adjectives for IG tone (e.g. direct, energetic, premium) | Reel Rules (Tone), Account-Specific Rules | Drives caption + script tone. |
| **Account roster** — brand handle plus founder/personal handles; for each: name, angle, "we" vs "I" voice | Podcast-to-Reels Pipeline, Account-Specific Rules | Determines who posts what and in which voice. |
| **Content pillars** — 3–5 recurring themes | Caption Patterns, Hashtag Strategy (Industry) | Rotate content across these. |
| **Key messages** — the 5–9 points captions and hooks reinforce | Caption Patterns (contrarian), Hashtag Strategy (Niche) | The points each caption should land. |
| **Differentiators** — what sets the brand apart | Carousel "Old Way vs New Way" (reference.md) | Used in contrast/contrarian carousels. |
| **Brand hashtags** — always-include tag(s): brand name, tagline, campaign | Hashtag Strategy (Brand) | Exact strings, on every post. |
| **Industry hashtags** — broad sector tags aligned to pillars | Hashtag Strategy (Industry) | Pick 3–4 per post. |
| **Niche hashtags** — narrow topic tags aligned to key messages | Hashtag Strategy (Niche) | Pick 2–3 per post. |
| **Location tags** — city/region hashtags when relevant | Hashtag Strategy (Location) | From your market context. |
| **Palette** — brand colors | Story Rules, Visual Standards (Colors) | Apply the brand-guidelines method. |
| **Typography** — display/body fonts | Visual Standards (Typography) | Clean, premium, not cluttered. |
| **Logo** — asset location + placement variants | Visual Standards (Logo), reel end cards | On every designed asset. |
| **Posting cadence** — reels/posts/stories per week per account | Instagram Content Types | Targets per account. |

## How to gather these

The numbered **Instagram Intake** in [`forms.md`](forms.md) is the questionnaire for all
of the above — ask it once, record the answers wherever your team keeps brand and content
decisions.

## Platform-independent (do not change)

The checklists, templates, and platform data are brand-neutral facts that work as-is for
any organization:
- `forms.md` — Reel Quality Checklist, Instagram Post Checklist, Reel Script Template,
  Weekly Instagram Plan Template, hashtag category structure.
- `reference.md` — reel script skeletons, carousel structures, story sequences, and the
  Platform-Specific Formatting table (aspect ratios, resolutions, caption limits).
