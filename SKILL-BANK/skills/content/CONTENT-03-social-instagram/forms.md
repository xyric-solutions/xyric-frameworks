# Instagram Content — Forms & Checklists

Ready-to-use intake, checklists, and templates. The brand voice, account roster,
content pillars, and hashtags are decided per organization (see [`ADAPT.md`](ADAPT.md));
this file never hardcodes them.

---

## Instagram Intake (the questionnaire for adapting this skill)

Ask as one numbered list and **WAIT** for answers; then record them wherever your team
keeps brand and content decisions. Ask only what's missing.

1. **Tagline & wordmark** — exact tagline string (incl. punctuation) for end cards, and
   the brand wordmark?
2. **Voice attributes** — 3–5 adjectives for the IG tone (e.g. direct, energetic, premium)?
3. **Accounts** — the brand handle plus any founder/personal handles, and for each: the
   person's name, their angle, and "we" vs "I" voice?
4. **Content pillars** — 3–5 recurring themes the content rotates through?
5. **Key messages** — the 5–9 points captions and hooks should reinforce?
6. **Brand hashtags** — the always-include tag(s) (brand name, tagline, campaign)? Record
   exact strings.
7. **Industry hashtags** — the broad sector tags aligned to the pillars?
8. **Niche hashtags** — narrow topic tags aligned to the key messages?
9. **Location tags** — city/region hashtags to use when relevant? From your market context.
10. **Posting cadence** — target reels/posts/stories per week per account?

## Reel Quality Checklist

```markdown
## Reel Check — [Account] — [Topic]

### Script Quality
- [ ] Hook in first 3 seconds — stops the scroll?
- [ ] One clear idea (not two or three)?
- [ ] Under 30 seconds (60s only for podcast clips)?
- [ ] Conversational tone — not a presentation? (matches the brand voice attributes)
- [ ] Ends with a clear takeaway or CTA?

### Production
- [ ] Vertical format (9:16)?
- [ ] Subtitles added (white text, brand font)?
- [ ] Fast cuts — new visual every 3-5s?
- [ ] Trending audio used (if applicable)?
- [ ] End card with brand logo + tagline (the exact brand tagline)?

### Brand
- [ ] Brand color overlays where appropriate (from the brand palette)?
- [ ] Logo on end card?
- [ ] Premium feel — not cluttered?
- [ ] Matches account voice (brand="we", founder accounts="I" — per your account roster)?

### Verdict: [ ] Ready to publish  [ ] Needs edit
```

## Instagram Post Checklist

```markdown
## Post Check — [Account] — [Date]

### Content
- [ ] Caption under 300 characters?
- [ ] First line is a hook (visible before "more")?
- [ ] Soft CTA or question included?
- [ ] Reinforces a key message (from your key messages)?
- [ ] Plain-language check passed (no filler/dead words)?

### Visual
- [ ] Correct aspect ratio (1:1 post, 9:16 reel)?
- [ ] Brand colors applied (from the brand palette)?
- [ ] Text on image under ~10 words per line?
- [ ] Logo on designed assets (subtle, bottom-right)?
- [ ] Real photos preferred over stock?

### Distribution
- [ ] 5-10 hashtags at end of caption?
- [ ] Brand hashtags included (your always-include tags)?
- [ ] Location tag added (if relevant)?
- [ ] People tagged in photo (if applicable)?
- [ ] Alt text added for accessibility?

### Cross-Posting
- [ ] Posted on correct account?
- [ ] Co-founder tagged (if collab)?
- [ ] Brand account tagged from personal accounts?
```
