# Adapting LinkedIn Content

**What this skill does:** writes professional, authoritative LinkedIn content (company-page
and founder posts) using premium positioning, pattern-based insight, and problem-focused
storytelling.

This skill is generic methodology — it never hardcodes a specific organization's voice,
pillars, audience, or messages. To use it for your organization, decide the values below and
either keep them in your content brief at use time, or bake them into your forked copy of
`SKILL.md`. Until they're decided, the skill emits a labeled GENERIC DRAFT (with
`[PILLAR]` / `[KEY MESSAGE]` / `[AUDIENCE TIER]` placeholders) rather than inventing them.

## What to decide for your organization

| Decide | Used in `SKILL.md` under | Notes |
|--------|--------------------------|-------|
| **Tagline & descriptor** — exact tagline string (incl. punctuation) + one-line descriptor | Philosophy | Never modify or translate the tagline. |
| **Voice attributes** — 3–5 adjectives defining how the brand sounds | Voice: company page vs founder | Applied to company-page voice. |
| **Off-limits** — words/clichés/styles to avoid beyond the universal Dead Words | Dead words | Merged with the universal kill list. |
| **Content pillars** — 3–5 recurring themes, each with its core insight | Content pillars, Content diversity | A pillar = theme + insight the brand owns. |
| **Channels** — which channels are active; is LinkedIn company + founder both in play | Posting cadence | Sets per-channel volume. |
| **Founders** — for founder posts: name, audience angle, personal voice per founder | Voice: company page vs founder, Posting cadence | Founder posts use "I"; company page uses "we". |
| **Audience tiers (ICP)** — each tier: name + description + message angle | Content pillars, Posting cadence | Time zones inferred from tiers drive best post times. |
| **Key messages & differentiators** — 5–9 messages to weave into posts | Philosophy, Content diversity | Phrase them exactly as decided; don't re-invent. |
| **Brand promise** — one core value statement | Philosophy | Used to sharpen insights, not announce. |

## How to gather these

The numbered **LinkedIn Content Intake** in [`forms.md`](forms.md) is the questionnaire for
all of the above — ask it once, record the answers wherever your team keeps brand and
content decisions.

## Platform-independent (do not change)

These are LinkedIn facts and writing craft — universal for any organization, no adaptation
needed:

- `SKILL.md`: post types, post structure, formatting guidelines, the 60/30/10 mix, the
  anti-pattern blocklist, and the universal Dead Words list.
- `forms.md`: the Quality Checklist, hashtag rules, post templates, success metrics, and the
  content-planning/monthly-review worksheets.
- `reference.md`: the format-diversity catalog, hook construction, anonymized case-study
  approach, full post examples, engagement guidelines, and post-timing windows.
