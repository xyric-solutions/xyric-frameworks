# Adapting Social Content - Twitter/X

**What this skill does:** writes punchy, authoritative Twitter/X posts and threads — hot
takes, contrarian insights, real-time commentary — in one organization's voice.

This skill is generic methodology — it never hardcodes a specific organization's voice or
topics. To use it for your organization, decide the values below and either keep them in
your content brief at use time, or bake them into your forked copy of `SKILL.md`. Until
they're decided, the skill emits a labeled GENERIC DRAFT rather than inventing a voice,
pillars, or messages.

## What to decide for your organization

| Decide | Used in `SKILL.md` under | Notes |
|--------|--------------------------|-------|
| **Tagline & descriptor** — exact tagline (incl. punctuation) + one-line "what we are" | Twitter/X Philosophy | North star for tone; mirror it, never paraphrase. |
| **Voice attributes** — 3-5 adjectives for the X voice (e.g. sharp, direct, confident) | Twitter vs. long-form social; Posting Strategy | Keeps tone consistent across posts. |
| **Avoid list** — words, clichés, or tones that are off-limits | Dead Words; Anti-Patterns | Adds to the universal Dead Words list. |
| **Content pillars** — the 3-5 recurring themes you post about | Content pillars (Twitter-adapted) | Every post maps to at least one. |
| **Audience tiers** — segments/ICP you speak to, each with a one-line description | Content pillars; Founder/team content | Each tweet should land for at least one tier. |
| **Key messages** — your 5-9 core messages / value statements | Content pillars (Twitter-adapted) | Tie each post to one. |
| **Differentiators** — what makes you different, one line each | Content pillars; Hot Takes Framework | Keep claims defensible. |
| **Active channels** — is X primary, equal, or secondary | Twitter/X Philosophy; Posting Strategy | Affects cadence and energy. |
| **Founder/team voices (optional)** — anyone posting personally, with angle + tone | Founder / team content on X | Leave empty to post only in the org voice. |
| **Content mix (optional)** — Core/Personality/Wildcard split (default 60/30/10) | Content Diversity | Override the percentages if your org differs. |

## How to gather these

The numbered **X Content Intake** in [`forms.md`](forms.md) is the questionnaire for all of
the above — ask it once, record the answers wherever your team keeps content decisions.

## Platform-independent (do not change)

`forms.md` checklists, templates, posting schedules, and success metrics, and `reference.md`
frameworks, formulas, and example structures are platform facts and neutral illustrations —
they work as-is for any organization. Only the example **topics** get swapped for your own
content pillars.
