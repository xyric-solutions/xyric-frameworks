## X Content Intake (the questionnaire for adapting this skill)

This is the questionnaire for the decisions in [`ADAPT.md`](ADAPT.md). Ask only what's
missing, as one numbered list, and **WAIT** for answers; then record them wherever your team
keeps content decisions.

1. **Tagline & descriptor** — exact tagline (incl. punctuation) and one-line "what we are"?
2. **Voice attributes** — 3-5 adjectives that describe the X voice (e.g. sharp, direct,
   confident)?
3. **Avoid list** — words, clichés, or tones that are off-limits?
4. **Content pillars** — the 3-5 recurring themes you post about?
5. **Audience tiers** — who you're speaking to (segments/ICP tiers), each with a one-line
   description?
6. **Key messages** — your 5-9 core messages / value statements?
7. **Differentiators** — what makes you different, in one line each?
8. **Active channels** — is X primary, equal, or secondary to other channels?
9. **Founders/team voices (optional)** — anyone posting in a personal voice, with their
   angle and tone?
10. **Content mix (optional)** — do you use a 60/30/10 Core/Personality/Wildcard split, or a
    different ratio?

## Quality Checklist

Before posting. "Brand X" / "your org" = your adapted values (see [`ADAPT.md`](ADAPT.md)).

### Human Check (mandatory)
- [ ] Sounds like a real person — not generic AI copy?
- [ ] At least ONE specific detail (name, number, place, year)?
- [ ] Would you actually tweet this from a personal account?
- [ ] First line punchy enough to stop the scroll?
- [ ] FREE of Dead Words and your avoid-list terms?

### Dead Words Check
- [ ] No "Leverage" (use "Use" or remove)
- [ ] No "Unlock" (use "Find" / "Build" / "Get")
- [ ] No "Game-changer" (say what actually changed)
- [ ] No "In today's fast-paced world" (delete)
- [ ] No "Let's dive in" / "Here's the thing" (delete, just start)
- [ ] No "Synergy" / "Holistic" / "Paradigm" (normal words)
- [ ] No "Excited to share" (just share it)
- [ ] No term from your avoid list

### Voice & Positioning
- [ ] Sounds confident, not desperate
- [ ] Would make someone stop and think
- [ ] Could only come from your org (not generic) — reflects your voice attributes
- [ ] Organization voice (we, not I) — unless a founder/team post per your founder voices
- [ ] Reinforces at least one of your differentiators or key messages

### Content Strategy Check
- [ ] Maps to an audience tier — who is this speaking to?
- [ ] Fits the content mix (Core / Personality / Wildcard, per your chosen ratio)
- [ ] Different format from the previous tweet (format diversity)
- [ ] One clear point or take
- [ ] Adds value to the timeline — not noise
- [ ] Would you RT this if someone else posted it?

### Anti-Pattern Check
- [ ] No engagement bait ("Repost if you agree")
- [ ] No hashtag stuffing (0-2 max)
- [ ] No empty hot takes (contrarian but defensible)
- [ ] No "Unpopular opinion: [popular opinion]" (dishonest framing)
- [ ] No Dead Words / avoid-list terms
- [ ] No same format twice in a row

### Format
- [ ] Mobile-readable
- [ ] 0-2 hashtags maximum
- [ ] Punchy first line
- [ ] No emoji overload (0-1 max)
- [ ] Single tweets ≤280 characters

## Thread Templates

### Hot Take Deep-Dive (5-8 tweets)

```
1/ [Controversial statement]

Here's why — and what to do about it:

[thread continues]

2/ [Context: Why most people think the opposite]

3/ [Evidence: What we've seen/experienced]

4/ [Deeper analysis: The nuance]

5/ [Implication: What this means for you]

6/ [Action: What to do about it]

7/ [Conclusion: Sharp takeaway]
```

### Pattern Story (6-10 tweets)

```
1/ A pattern we've seen [X] times:
[Brief setup]
[thread continues]

2/ What they say: [Common request]
What we hear: [Underlying problem]

3/ Step 1: [First action]

4/ The pattern: [What we consistently find]

5/ The fix: [Our approach]

6/ The result: [Specific outcomes]

7/ The lesson: [Broader takeaway]
```

### Framework Share (4-6 tweets)

```
1/ [Problem statement]
There's a framework that fixes this:
[thread continues]

2/ [Framework name/concept]
[Brief explanation]

3/ Step 1: [First element]

4/ Step 2: [Second element]

5/ Step 3: [Third element]

6/ The result: [What changes when you apply this]
```

## Hashtag Reference

### When to Use
- Joining specific conversations (a topical or community hashtag for your domain)
- Industry topics trending
- Maximum 1-2 per tweet

### When to Skip
- Hot takes (no hashtags needed)
- Most regular tweets
- Threads (use in first tweet only if relevant)

> Use hashtags relevant to your domain community — pull candidate tags from your
> key-message topics or content pillars. Keep them few and specific.

## Posting Schedule Template

Adjust times to your audience's primary time zone(s).

### Daily Rhythm (full cadence)

| Time slot | Type |
|-----------|------|
| **Morning** | Hot takes, observations, one-liners |
| **Midday** | Industry react, quote tweets |
| **Evening** | Threads, debate starters |

### Weekly Content Mix (full cadence)

| Type | Frequency | Mix Bucket |
|------|-----------|------------|
| Hot takes / one-liners | 4-5/week | Core (60%) |
| Industry reactions / QRTs | 2-3/week | Core |
| Threads (deep-dive, pattern, framework) | 2-3/week | Core |
| Quick polls | 1/week | Personality / Wildcard |
| Weekend reflective | 1/week | Personality (30%) |
| Off-topic / trending | 1/week | Wildcard (10%) |

| Type | Frequency |
|------|-----------|

| Threads | 1/week |
| Poll or debate starter | 1/week |

## Success Metrics

### Engagement Quality

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Engagement rate | 2-4%+ |
| Follower growth | Trending up (quality over quantity) |
| Quote tweets | Others amplifying your takes |
| Inbound inquiries | Ultimate goal |

### High-Quality Signals
- Replies that engage with the idea
- Quote tweets adding perspective
- Saves/bookmarks on threads
- Followers who are decision-makers in your target tiers

### Low-Quality Signals
- Generic "great take" replies
- Bot or engagement-pod followers
- Likes without meaningful engagement
