# Adapting Proposal Generator

**What this skill does:** drafts client-centric consulting proposals that lead with the
client's business pain and a phased partnership journey, and reviews proposals for that
same client-centricity.

This skill is generic methodology — it never hardcodes a specific firm. To use it for your
organization, decide the values below and either keep them in your proposal brief at use
time, or bake them into your forked copy of `SKILL.md`. Until they're decided, the skill
emits a labeled GENERIC DRAFT with `PLACEHOLDER` values rather than inventing a firm name,
promise, service line, price, or outcome metric.

## What to decide for your organization

| Decide | Used in `SKILL.md` under | Notes |
|--------|--------------------------|-------|
| **Firm name & wordmark** — exact strings incl. punctuation | Firm & offering inputs; proposal template "Prepared by" / signature | Used verbatim; never improvised. |
| **Descriptor** — one-line "what we are" | Firm & offering inputs | Optional positioning line. |
| **Promise** — core value statement every engagement makes | Firm & offering inputs; "Why [Firm]" block | — |
| **Key messages** — 5-9 points to land in any proposal (e.g. "one partner for the whole journey", "partner not vendor") | Firm & offering inputs; "Why [Firm]" block | — |
| **Differentiators** — what makes the firm different | Firm & offering inputs; "Why [Firm]" block | The "why us" content. |
| **Business model** — one line (e.g. phased projects + retainer) | Pricing philosophy | — |
| **Service lines** — for each: name, one-line summary, and the **client pain it solves** (in client language, NO internal code) | Client problem bundles; Phased engagement framework | Drives the pain → service-line mapping in `reference.md`. |
| **Who prepares / signs** — title and contact line for the signature block | Firm & offering inputs | Supplied per proposal. |
| **Pricing & outcome metrics** | Pricing philosophy; proposal template | **HUMAN INPUT per proposal** — never decided once, never invented. |

## How to gather these

The numbered **Proposal Intake** in [`forms.md`](forms.md) is the questionnaire for all the
firm-specific values above — ask it once, record the answers wherever your team keeps these
decisions. The **Discovery Recap** in `forms.md` is the per-client questionnaire (pain,
current-state cost, desired outcome, constraints, confirmed pricing) — run it for every
proposal.

## Platform-independent (do not change)

`forms.md` (the proposal template, the intake and discovery questionnaires, and the quality
checklist) and `reference.md` (the four-phase methodology, the engagement/retainer structure,
and the ROI-framing patterns) are firm-neutral facts — they work as-is for any consulting
firm. Pricing is always HUMAN INPUT, never baked in.
