# Client Communication

Write client emails that sound like a team member working alongside the client, not a
consultant delivering to them. This skill is the **method** — voice, structure, tone
adaptation, anti-patterns, and templates. The specifics it operates on — the sender's name,
the brand voice, and the partnership positioning — are decided per organization. See
[`ADAPT.md`](ADAPT.md) for what to decide before using this for a given organization.

When those specifics are not yet decided, **do not invent them** — produce a clearly-labeled
**GENERIC DRAFT** with neutral `PLACEHOLDER` values (e.g. `[Sender]`, `[Your Org]`) and list
what's missing. Never invent the sender's name, the organization's brand voice, a signature,
or a positioning claim.

Resolve the sign-off name from the organization's sender name (or the signing individual when
a person signs), match the tone to the organization's brand voice attributes, honor its
avoid list, and frame value using its partnership promise and differentiators.

## Core philosophy: "We're your team, not your vendor."

| Principle | What it means | How to execute |
|-----------|---------------|----------------|
| **Client sets the pace** | Cadence adapts to them | Daily, weekly, or async — whatever they prefer |
| **Embedded partner** | Sound like a team member, not an outsider | "We found" not "We discovered for you" |
| **Personalization first** | Every email is tailored | No copy-paste templates without customization |

### Partnership voice

Default to the partner voice; tune the exact wording to the brand voice attributes.

| Vendor voice | Partner voice |
|--------------|---------------|
| "We have completed the deliverable for your review" | "The dashboard's ready — here's what it unlocks for you" |
| "Please advise on next steps" | "I'm thinking we tackle X next. Sound right?" |
| "If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to reach out" | "Questions? Let's jump on a quick call" |
| "As your service provider, we recommend..." | "Based on what I've seen in your data, I'd suggest..." |

## When to activate

**Triggers**:
- "Write a client email about [topic]"
- "Draft email to [client]"
- "Help me respond to this client message"
- Client updates, proposals, follow-ups, issue resolution

## Email categories

| Category | Purpose | Tone |
|----------|---------|------|
| **Progress updates** | Status, milestones | Informative, collaborative |
| **Deliverable submissions** | Sharing work products | Confident, helpful |
| **Meeting requests** | Scheduling | Respectful of their time |
| **Issue / blocker** | Problems, solutions | Proactive, solution-focused |
| **Follow-ups** | Checking in | Non-pushy, value-add |
| **Scope changes** | New work, adjustments | Collaborative, clear |
| **Closing / handover** | Project wrap-up | Warm, forward-looking |

## AI-cringe anti-patterns

| Phrase | Instead |
|--------|---------|
| "I hope this email finds you well" | Jump straight to the point |
| "Per my last email..." | "Circling back on..." |
| "Please advise" | "Let me know your thoughts" |
| "Please don't hesitate to reach out" | "Questions? Just reply" |
| "Touching base" | State the actual purpose |
| "I wanted to reach out regarding..." | Start with the point |
| "At your earliest convenience" | Specific date or "when you get a chance" |

Also drop anything on the organization's avoid list (off-limits clichés or words).

## Tone adaptation guide

### By relationship stage

| Stage | Greeting | Sign-off | Formality |
|-------|----------|----------|-----------|
| **Pre-engagement** | "Hi [Name]" | "Best, [Sender]" | Professional, warm |
| **Early project** | "Hey [Name]" | "[Sender]" | Professional, friendly |
| **Deep in project** | "Hey [Name]" or just start | "[Sender]" or "-[Initial]" | Casual, efficient |
| **Long-term partner** | First name or start direct | Initial or nothing | Very casual |

`[Sender]` resolves from the organization's sender name (or the signing individual).

### By urgency

| Urgency | Subject line | Tone | Length |
|---------|--------------|------|--------|
| **Urgent** | Start with "URGENT:" | Direct, clear, action-focused | Short |
| **Normal** | Standard format | Balanced | As needed |
| **FYI only** | Include "FYI:" or "(no action needed)" | Light, informative | Brief |

## Response time guidelines

Set the actual targets from the organization's stated service standard; these are sensible defaults.

| Email type | Target response |
|------------|-----------------|
| Urgent / issue reported | Within 2 hours |
| Question needing an answer | Same business day |
| FYI / update | Acknowledge within 24 hours |
| Decision request | Within 24 hours (even if "need more time") |
