# ICP & UCP Targeting — Forms & Checklists

Ready-to-use intake, targeting forms, qualification checklist, and coverage audit. The
audience and positioning specifics are decided per organization (see [`ADAPT.md`](ADAPT.md));
this file never hardcodes them. Where a template shows `[Tier name]` or `[Pillar name]`, fill
it from your own ICP tiers / UCP pillars.

---

## Positioning Intake (the questionnaire for adapting this skill)

This is the intake for adapting the skill to an organization. Ask as one numbered list and
**WAIT** for answers; then record them wherever your team keeps positioning decisions. Ask
only what's missing.

1. **Brand promise** — your one-line core value statement?
2. **ICP tiers** — name each audience tier you serve, and for each: who they are (roles),
   firmographics (size/stage/industry), and core pain. **No pricing** — keep it about
   who/pain (each tier: `{ name, description }`).
3. **Shared psychographics** — traits common to all your tiers (mindset, buying triggers,
   what they're allergic to)?
4. **ICP anti-patterns** — who you deliberately do NOT serve, and why?
5. **UCP pillars** — your competitive-positioning pillars. For each: a name, a one-line
   meaning, and a concrete proof point (each pillar: `{ name, meaning, proof }`). If you'd
   rather, list your differentiators instead.
6. **Key messages** — 5–9 canonical, pre-approved messages, each with its best context?
7. **Per-tier messaging** — for each tier, the message that resonates vs. the message that
   falls flat?
8. **Tier coverage targets** (optional) — desired % of content per tier across a quarter?

## Quick Targeting Decision Tree

Adapt the leaves to your own ICP tiers.

```
Is this content for a specific client/lead?
├── YES → Identify their tier → Use tier-specific messaging
└── NO → General content — route by intent:
    ├── Thought leadership   → decision-maker tiers
    ├── Origin / founder story → founder-relatable tiers
    ├── Proof / speed story  → all tiers
    ├── Personal / how-to tips → individual / smallest tier
    ├── Case study           → buyer / decision-maker tiers
    └── Behind-the-scenes    → small-team / founder tiers
```
