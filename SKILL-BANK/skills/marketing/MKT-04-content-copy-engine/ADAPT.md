# Adapting Content Copy Engine

**What this skill does:** turns a topic and a raw draft into ready-to-post social
copy, profile copy, and design briefs — selecting a copy template, fitting the story
in, and validating every piece through a human-voice check, a LinkedIn growth/hook
system, and a founder-authenticity gate.

This skill is generic methodology — the templates, hooks, gates, and workflow are
universal. To use it for your organization, decide the values below and either keep
them in your content brief at use time, or bake them into your forked copy of
`SKILL.md`. Until they're decided, the skill emits a labeled GENERIC DRAFT (with
`PLACEHOLDER` values) rather than inventing your key messages, pillars, palette,
tagline, or a founder's story.

## What to decide for your organization

| Decide | Used in `SKILL.md` under | Notes |
|--------|--------------------------|-------|
| **Accounts** — which accounts post (the org + any named founders) | When to Activate, Content Generation Workflow (IDENTIFY) | Capture a one-line voice note per founder. |
| **Brand promise** — one-line core value statement | LinkedIn Growth Engine, Brand block | Drives the "who you help / problem / how" line. |
| **Key messages** — your 5–9 recurring messages (one is the signature theme) | Hook Rules, Quick Template Picker, Targeting | Signature theme becomes a recurring hook. |
| **Differentiators / value pillars** — what makes you different | LinkedIn Growth Engine, Targeting | Used for value-pillar tagging. |
| **ICP tiers** — audience segments you target (name + one line each, no pricing) | Content Generation Workflow (TARGET), Output Format | Every post maps to a tier. |
| **Content pillars** — your 3–5 content pillars | Strategy inputs, Hook Rules, Targeting | Used for hook keywords + pillar assignment. |
| **Channels** — active platforms (LinkedIn / X / Instagram / etc.) | When to Activate, Strategy inputs | Filters which platform rules apply. |
| **Voice** — 3–5 voice adjectives + a words-to-avoid list | Human Check, Dead Words | Words-to-avoid stack on top of the baseline dead-words list. |
| **Tagline & descriptor** — exact tagline string + one-line "what we are" | Brand block | Use the tagline exactly as written. |
| **Brand palette** — primary / accent / light / dark tokens | Design Brief Format, Output Format | For design briefs; never hardcode hex. |
| **Org name** — organization or product name | Brand block | For the "who we are" line. |
| **Content output location** — where finished copy is filed | When to Activate (TARGET workflow) | e.g. your content folder or per-channel tracker. |

## How to gather these

The numbered **Content Context Intake** in [`forms.md`](forms.md) is the
questionnaire for all of the above — ask it once and record the answers wherever your
team keeps brand and content decisions.

## Platform-independent (do not change)

The following are universal facts and need no adaptation:

- `forms.md` checklists (Human Check, LinkedIn Growth Check, Founder Personal Touch,
  Quality Gate) and output templates.
- `reference.md` starter copy-template library, LinkedIn growth playbook,
  before/after transformation patterns, and the copy-length reference (platform
  character limits).
- The Dead Words baseline list in `SKILL.md` (your words-to-avoid list adds to it).
