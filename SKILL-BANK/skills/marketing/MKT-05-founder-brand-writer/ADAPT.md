# Adapting Founder Brand Writer

**What this skill does:** writes personal-brand content in each founder's distinct voice —
solo posts, co-founder collaborations, and cross-promotion with the company brand.

This skill is generic methodology — it never hardcodes a specific founder or brand. To use it
for your organization, decide the values below and either keep them in your content brief at
use time, or bake them into your forked copy of `SKILL.md`. Until they're decided, the skill
emits a labeled GENERIC DRAFT (e.g. "Founder A", "[ANGLE]") rather than inventing a person.

## What to decide for your organization

| Decide | Used in `SKILL.md` under | Notes |
|--------|--------------------------|-------|
| **Founder roster** — one entry per founder | The founders / "Founder A / B" labels | "Founder A / B" = first and second founders; >2 founders apply pairwise. |
| **Per founder: name** — full public name | The founders | Real person — never invent. |
| **Per founder: role** — one-line role | The founders (Role) | e.g. "the expert who builds". |
| **Per founder: angle** — distinct lens vs. the other(s) | The founders (Angle); pillar focus | Must be complementary, not duplicative. |
| **Per founder: background** — career/domains, employers, ventures | The founders (Background) | Source of real stories — never fabricate. |
| **Per founder: core message** — the gap/problem they name | The founders (Core message) | — |
| **Per founder: content tone** — how they sound | The founders (Content tone) | e.g. methodology-driven vs. ROI-driven. |
| **Per founder: audience** — who they speak to | The founders (Audience) | Titles / segments. |
| **Per founder: voice adjectives** — 3-5 words | The founders (Voice); human-voice policy | The signature fingerprint to preserve. |
| **Per founder: credibility line** — one-line proof of authority | The founders (Credibility) | — |
| **Per founder: stage pitch** — one-line keynote pitch | The founders (Stage pitch) | — |
| **Per founder: pillar split** — content-pillar % | Content pillar distribution | Defaults to 35/30/20/15 if undefined. |
| **Per founder: strategy docs** — branding doc + GTM/schedule doc locations | Strategy references | Path or link; never hardcode in the skill. |
| **Marketing philosophy doc** — content idea bank + core/personality/wildcard mix | Content mix; Strategy references | Defaults to 60/30/10 if undefined. |
| **Company brand voice** — tagline (exact string), tone attributes, what to avoid | Co-founder collaboration; Cross-promotion | Tagline used only as written; never modify. |
| **Positioning promise** — brand promise / core value | Cross-promotion (brand-proof identity) | Founders demonstrate it, never sloganeer it. |

## How to gather these

The numbered **Founder Identity Intake** in [`forms.md`](forms.md) is the questionnaire for
the per-founder fields and the org-level docs above — ask it once per founder, and record the
answers wherever your team keeps founder and brand decisions. Founder bios, personal stories,
and credentials are private to the org — keep them out of the shared skill.

## Platform-independent (do not change)

`forms.md` checklists and templates (voice validation, growth/intro checklists, debate and
weekly-plan templates, engagement tracker) and `reference.md` patterns (growth post structure,
voice-comparison examples, platform cadence) are method-neutral — they work as-is for any
organization. Only the bracketed founder/brand values get filled in per the table above.
