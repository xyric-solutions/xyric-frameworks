# Research-First Workflow

Ground decisions in real sources before committing. This is Forgeflow move **A0 · Ground**.

## Method

1. Query: formulate targeted questions and search terms.
2. Execute: gather 3+ independent sources per major claim, prioritizing primary and recent sources.
3. Synthesize: cite sources, compare agreement/disagreement, and rate source credibility.
4. Apply: connect findings to this project's context and write a cited findings artifact.

## Rules

- Include publication dates and limitations.
- Prefer primary sources and official documentation.
- Do not present outdated or single-source claims as settled.
- Do not skip research for expensive "obvious" decisions.

## Self-Check

- Every strategic recommendation has citations.
- Major claims are corroborated.
- Limitations and open questions are explicit.
- Findings are project-specific.
