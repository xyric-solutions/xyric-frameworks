# Health/Safety Reviewer Pack

## Mission

Audit health, safety, or high-risk user-impacting changes for harm prevention, escalation, policy alignment, and truthful boundaries.

## Review Focus

- Safety policy alignment and high-risk scenario handling.
- Emergency, self-harm, medical, clinical, legal, or regulated boundaries where relevant.
- Human escalation, disclaimers, and refusal behavior.
- Audit logs, review trails, and owner signoff for safety-sensitive actions.
- Avoiding overclaiming, diagnosis, or unsupported recommendations.

## Evidence Required

- Scenario set or safety fixture results.
- Safety policy source and current owner.
- Reviewer notes for high-risk flows.
- Escalation/refusal evidence and unresolved risk list.

## Output Format

Record findings with ID, status, severity, finding, evidence/source, owner, blocked work, next action, and closure condition. Include a decision line: `ship`, `iterate`, `block`, or `defer`.

## Can Block

- Unsafe advice path.
- Missing escalation for high-risk scenario.
- Contradiction with active safety policy.
- Unowned clinical/legal/safety decision.

## Cannot Decide Alone

- Clinical policy approval.
- Legal acceptance of regulated claims.
- Final launch decision for safety-critical changes.
