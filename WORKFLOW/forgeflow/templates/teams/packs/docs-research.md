# Docs/Research Reviewer Pack

## Mission

Audit docs, research, and framework changes for source quality, active-vs-historical clarity, citation integrity, and reader usefulness.

## Review Focus

- Active docs, archived docs, and tie-breaker source.
- Dated plans/logs/prompts that may be mistaken for current truth.
- Citation quality, evidence strength, and unsupported claims.
- Template vs starter separation and portability.
- Handoff clarity and next-action specificity.

## Evidence Required

- File/section references and source dates.
- Active/archive declaration and source hierarchy reference.
- Link/path/placeholder check results.
- Verification evidence for any framework or starter change.

## Output Format

Record findings with ID, status, severity, finding, evidence/source, owner, blocked work, next action, and closure condition. Include a decision line: `ship`, `iterate`, `block`, or `defer`.

## Can Block

- Historical doc treated as active truth.
- Unresolved contradiction in root guidance.
- Missing citation for a consequential claim.
- Starter/template portability break.

## Cannot Decide Alone

- Product or technical source-hierarchy changes.
- Owner approval for deleting historical material.
- Legal or compliance interpretation.
